# 🏗️ Bidii Quality Builders System

## 📌 Project Overview

The Bidii Quality Builders System is a web-based construction management platform designed to digitize and automate the operational workflow of Bidii Quality Builders. The system is developed using the Django web framework and Python, following secure coding practices and principles of Lean Software Development.

The primary problem addressed by this system is the inefficiency of manual processes used in managing construction projects. These manual methods result in poor data management, delays in service delivery, lack of transparency, and increased risk of human error.

This system provides a centralized, scalable, and efficient solution that integrates all business processes into a single platform, enabling better coordination, tracking, and decision-making.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Bidii Quality Builders currently faces the following challenges:

* Manual recording of customer and project details
* Delays in generating and delivering estimates
* Inefficient job scheduling and tracking
* Lack of structured invoicing and payment management
* Difficulty in monitoring business performance
* Risk of data loss and inconsistencies

---

## ✅ Solution

The system introduces a fully integrated digital platform that automates the lifecycle of construction projects—from initial customer contact to final payment. It ensures data consistency, improves operational efficiency, and enhances service delivery through real-time access to information.

---

## ⚙️ Core Features

### 1. Customer Management

* Capture and manage customer details (name, contact, email)
* Maintain a history of all customer projects

### 2. Project & Job Management

* Create and manage construction projects
* Schedule job start dates based on workload
* Track job progress and status

### 3. Estimate Management

* Generate detailed cost estimates for projects
* Record estimate creation date and amount
* Support customer approval workflow

### 4. Material Planning

* Manage required construction materials
* Support planning for material ordering before job start

### 5. Invoice Generation

* Automatically generate invoices after job completion
* Record total cost and issue date
* Maintain invoice history

### 6. Payment Tracking

* Record customer payments
* Track outstanding balances
* Monitor payment deadlines (30-day period)

### 7. Dashboard & Data Visualization

* Visualize key business metrics using Matplotlib
* Revenue trends over time
* Number of completed vs ongoing projects
* Pending payments overview

### 8. Admin Panel (Django)

* Secure login for system administrator (proprietor)
* Full control over all system data
* CRUD operations for all entities

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture

The system follows a **Model-View-Template (MVT)** architecture provided by Django:

* **Models** → Define database structure (Customer, Project, Estimate, Invoice, Payment)
* **Views** → Handle business logic and request processing
* **Templates** → Render user interface

This architecture ensures separation of concerns, scalability, and maintainability.
